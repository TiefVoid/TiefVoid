Learn about the regions

For as small as Khordad may be, there is a surprising range of biomes that have divided the distribution of settlements naturally. Here we include further details by the pen of renowned historian Erlan Tushe.

Northwestern Moors

Rolling hills blanketed in mauve are home to a few settlements especialized in livestock and hunting.

Read more

Afanasiy Range

A mountain range to the Northeast of Khordad where mining and agriculture are the most prolific.

Read more

Western Swamplands

A forsaken land where people do their best to get by, and the best place to get any potion component or concoction one might need.

Read more

Central Valley

The valley frames the Niloufar river and houses the imperial city, as well as its immediate subservient settlements.

Read more

Eastern Prairies

Flat lands as far as the eye can reach, this region covers most of the Eastern side of Khordad.

Read more

Userkaf Desert

Desolate land surrounds a sprawling walled city and gives way for the largest comercial port of Khordad.

Read more

Southern Seas

Treacherous waters not fit for far travel, if not for the shallows that lead to Lestari Island.
