Government

From royals to politicians

Divine Designation

The Farr is the divine favor bestowed upon those chosen to rule over the continent of Khordad.

Other regions handle their governmental appointments by more conventional methods, although the notion of the Farr does permeate other customs.

Imperial Line

The imperial city of Minoo has seen emperors and empresses come and go in all its history. Each has adopted a title that describes their ruling, although some have only been determined once their time is done. It is common practice to refer to them by this title and not directly their name as a sign of respect; no matter how wicked they may have been, they were still blessed.

Other forms of power

While the power is centralized in the imperial city of Minoo, other regions are far apart enough that there is a delicate balance authority wise. No one cares for the godforsaken swamps, the Dwarven Triumvirate of Salzwedel and the Elven Council of Genrikh essentially do their own thing, the Tower of Inanna in the expansive Eastern Prairies is an uncomfortable mystery, and the walled city of Akhenaton has isolated its population from the world.
